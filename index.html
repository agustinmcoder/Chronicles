Rules & Guide

Objective

First player to defeat 3 Final Bosses wins the game.

Movement

Roll D6, click a highlighted tile to move. You cannot revisit a tile you stood on in the last 8 turns. Drag to pan the map.

Classes & Skills

🧙 Mage: Basic Attack, Lighting Bolt, Fireball, Curse, Healing Beam, Defend, Run

🏹 Ranger: Shoot Arrow, Double Arrow, Healing Beam, Defend, Swift Retreat, Call of the Nature

⚔️ Warrior: Basic Attack, Fierce Attack (recharges 1 turn), Magic Bolt, Defend (75% with shield), Run

🥊 Monk: Basic Attack, Furious Blows (1–6 rapid hits), Magic Bolt, Defensive Stance (75% next hit), Run

🗡️ Rogue: Basic Attack, Backstab, Poison Blade, Smoke Bomb, Evasion, Sneak Attack, Shadow Cloak, Run

Shop

3 equipment items for your class, plus a 50% chance each for HP and Mana Potions.

Cards

Common · Rare · Epic · Legendary. Hover any card to see full stats. Click player in HUD to open inventory.
